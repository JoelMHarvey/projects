Why airgap?

The scroll does nothing

Forty minutes on your phone and you have nothing to show for it. An hour at a workbench and you have a stitch, a chord, a joint that fits.

Skills are cumulative

Every session builds on the last. After six months of guitar you can play songs. After six months of scrolling you can scroll faster.

The hard part is starting

What tools? What materials? How do I not waste money on rubbish? We answer that before you spend a penny.

Mastery is the point

We don't stop at "try it once." Every course maps the full road — beginner, competent, good, excellent. You choose how far to go.
