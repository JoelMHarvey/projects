==== Script 1 — "The bench" (origin) ====
save as: voiceover/origin-bench-001.mp3
[Direction: personal, honest, a little angry but calm. This is your story — slow down, mean it.]

I was training to be a counsellor when I found out something that shocked me.  On the big platforms a client can pay seventy to a hundred dollars a week, while the therapist doing the work might see around a third of it.  The platform pockets the rest. For providing a platform.  So I built something else. No commission on sessions. Free to start. You set your own prices and keep what you charge.  The only per-session cost is standard card processing — and Faresay keeps none of it.  I built the thing I want to use myself. If you're a therapist, come have a look.


==== Script 2 — "$7.8 million" (data betrayal) ====

[Direction: matter-of-fact, let the facts land. Slight pause before ‘$7.8 million’ and before ‘After promising it wouldn’t.’]

A therapy app promised to keep your secrets. Then it got fined $7.8 million.  In 2023 the US FTC ordered BetterHelp to pay $7.8 million.  The reason: sharing users' sensitive mental-health data with Facebook for advertising.  After promising it wouldn't.  The people who came to that app were asking for help. Not to be targeted.  Your data should stay yours. That's the whole idea behind Faresay.
